Small images should be shown at their actual size.

![Spruce shoot close-up](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/9/9e/Picea_abies_shoot_with_buds%2C_Sogndal%2C_Norway.jpg/240px-Picea_abies_shoot_with_buds%2C_Sogndal%2C_Norway.jpg)

Large images should always scale down and fit in the content container.

![Large spruce shoot photo](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/9/9e/Picea_abies_shoot_with_buds%2C_Sogndal%2C_Norway.jpg/1024px-Picea_abies_shoot_with_buds%2C_Sogndal%2C_Norway.jpg)
